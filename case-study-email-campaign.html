Case Study

Just By Texting
You Can Launch Email Campaigns

Type a message in Telegram. The AI understands your intent, finds the right customers or suppliers from your database, writes the email, and sends it — automatically. No dashboard, no templates, no manual work.

  • Type one message — reach every customer who bought a specific product
  • Send BOGO, discount, or restock alerts to the exact right people
  • Suppliers notified automatically — one product, multiple suppliers handled in one go
  • AI writes the email body in the right tone: promo for customers, B2B for suppliers
Before & After

From manual chaos to one text message

Before
  • Owner manually exports customer list from spreadsheet
  • Writes email copy from scratch every time
  • Sends bulk email with no product-level targeting
  • Contacts each supplier one by one via phone or email
  • Hours wasted per campaign, errors common
After
  • Owner types a single message in Telegram
  • AI identifies product ID and target audience
  • Database queried — only relevant customers fetched
  • All linked suppliers notified in the same flow
  • Every email sent via Gmail. Zero human involvement.
How It Works

From one text to sent emails in seconds

Step 1
You type in Telegram

Send a plain-text message like "Send 20% discount email for Product X to all buyers." No special syntax. No form to fill. Just speak naturally.

Step 2
AI reads intent & queries DB

The system extracts the product ID, identifies audience type (customers vs suppliers), and fetches only the relevant contacts from your database — nobody else.

Step 3
Emails written & sent via Gmail

An LLM writes a context-aware subject line and body — promotional tone for customers, B2B tone for suppliers — then fires every email through Gmail instantly. Done.
