What Changed

Before this system, every missed call was a lost patient. Now the AI agent handles every inbound call, checks doctor availability in real time, books the slot, and fires an SMS confirmation - all without a single human involved.

Revenue

Clinics went from losing patients to missed calls, to recovering $10,000+ per month in booked appointments.

Automation

The AI agent books, reschedules, and cancels appointments autonomously - staff no longer touch the phone.

Reliability

Every call gets a response. Every booking writes to the database. Every patient gets an SMS confirmation code.
