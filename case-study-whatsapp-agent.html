Frequently Asked Questions

Details on the architecture

Q1: What happens if a customer asks about a product the shop doesn't carry?

A: As an advanced AI agent for retail shop India, the system uses Pinecone vector search to find the closest matching product semantically. If nothing relevant is found, it responds honestly and suggests alternatives from the catalog.

Q2: How does complaint detection work?

A: To automate WhatsApp customer queries safely, our AI scans the message for negative sentiment - "broken," "delayed," "wrong." If any are detected, it triggers the automatic complaint escalation system to notify your team with recommended follow-up actions.

Q3: Does the customer need to use any special commands or format their message?

A: No. The customer types natively as they would to any person. It is a seamless WhatsApp bot for computer shop that relies purely on natural language understanding, completely invisible to the buyer.

Q4: Where does the product, cart, and order data live?

A: All data lives securely in your Google Sheets CRM - one sheet each for products, carts, orders, and FAQs. This powers 24/7 WhatsApp support automation locally, requiring absolutely zero external database setup.

Q5: Can this work for shops other than PC hardware?

A: Yes. The product catalog, rules, and logic are all adaptable. You can reduce missed customer messages and scale conversions for any product-led business leaning on WhatsApp as a channel.
