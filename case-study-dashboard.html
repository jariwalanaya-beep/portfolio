How It Works

From Sheet to dashboard in a day

Step 1

Connect your Sheet. Share your Google Sheet link. That is it — no exports, no CSV uploads, no reformatting.

Step 2

We map your data. The system reads your columns sales, expenses, staff, inventory and auto-assigns the right charts and KPIs.

Step 3

Your dashboard goes live in under 24 hours, ready for your whole team to open on any device.
