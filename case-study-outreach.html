Email Sequence

The outreach system that follows up for you

1
You set it up once from the dashboard

Open the outreach dashboard, enter a prospect’s name and email, write your 6 messages, choose a subject line, and pick a send date and time. That’s it. Everything from here runs on its own.

2
The system remembers every contact

All 6 messages are saved against that contact — along with where they are in the sequence and when the next message is due. No spreadsheet to manage, no calendar reminders to set.

3
It checks the clock every hour

Every hour, the system looks at every active contact and asks: "Is anyone due a message right now?" If yes, it sends. If no, it waits. You never need to think about timing again.

4
Emails go out from your own Inbox — as replies

The first email starts a fresh conversation. Every follow-up after that is a reply in the same thread — so the prospect sees one ongoing conversation, not six separate emails. Sent from your Gmail, not a third-party tool.

Day 0 — First touch Day 3 — Follow-up Day 6 — Value add Day 10 — Case study Day 16 — Different angle Day 23 — Final note
5
Sequence ends cleanly — nothing falls through the cracks

After the last message, the conversation is automatically tagged as complete in your inbox. You always know who’s been fully followed up and who hasn’t. No guessing, no loose ends.
