Email Sequence

The outreach system that follows up for you

1
You set it up once from the dashboard

Open the outreach dashboard, enter a prospect's name and email, write your 6 messages, choose a subject line, and pick a send date. Everything from here runs on its own.

2
The system remembers every contact

All 6 messages are saved against that contact - along with where they are in the sequence and when the next message is due. No spreadsheet, no calendar reminders.

3
It checks the clock every hour

Every hour the system asks: "Is anyone due a message right now?" If yes, it sends. If no, it waits. You never need to think about timing again.

4
Emails go out from your own Inbox - as replies

The first email starts a fresh conversation. Every follow-up is a reply in the same thread - so the prospect sees one ongoing conversation, not six separate emails.

Day 0 - First touch Day 3 - Follow-up Day 6 - Value add Day 10 - Case study Day 16 - Different angle Day 23 - Final note
5
Sequence ends cleanly - nothing falls through

After the last message, the conversation is tagged complete in your dashboard. You always know who's been fully followed up and who hasn't. No guessing, no loose ends.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before deploying the outreach system for your business.

Still have questions? Book a call →
Does the AI really write a unique opener for every prospect?
▼
Yes. The AI reads each prospect's LinkedIn profile, recent posts, bio, and company page before writing the opener. Each message references something specific to that person - not a template with a name swapped in.
Will my Gmail account get flagged for spam?
▼
The system sends from your actual Gmail account using the Gmail API - not a third-party IP. Emails go out as replies in threads, which is how normal human conversations work. Volume is managed automatically to stay within safe daily limits.
What platforms can it scrape prospects from?
▼
LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook pages, YouTube channels, and Google Search results - all from a single form submission. You can target each platform separately or run them in parallel.
Where does my data go?
▼
All leads and email history are stored in your own Google Sheet and a Postgres database that you control. Nothing goes into a third-party CRM or shared database. You own the data entirely.
Can I stop the sequence if a prospect replies?
▼
Yes. When a prospect replies, you can immediately mark them as active in the dashboard and the system will pause further automated follow-ups for that contact. You take over the conversation manually from there.
